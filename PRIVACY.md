# Privacy

Poké Price Lens does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The extension reads Pokémon card names, numbers, set names, and the current product URL from supported pages. It sends direct requests from your browser to PriceCharting to retrieve public pricing pages. Selected English/Japanese matches and exact Collectr or TCGplayer product URLs you visit are stored locally with `chrome.storage.local` so the extension can remember your choices and provide direct navigation later.

No analytics, advertising, remote code, accounts, or extension-operated servers are used.
